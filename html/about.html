What is Crop Doctor all about?

Crops are the most essential source of food for humans.

And in order to feed the growing population, it is essential to keep them healthy and pests free.

Crop Doctor is a website that helps you detect the exact disease in your crops along with the appropriate measures to keep them healthy.

You just need to select the crop you want to detect the disease in from the given options.

Then upload the picture of the diseased crop from your system in order to detect it and you are good to go!
