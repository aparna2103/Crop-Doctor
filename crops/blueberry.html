Blueberry

Blueberry crop prefers soils which are highly acidic, fertile, aerated, moist and well-drained. The optimal soil pH range is 4.0 to 5.5 for best growth and yield.
In case higher pH, adding a small amount of sulfur to the soil can reduce the pH of the soil. Before starting a blueberry cultivation, go for soil testing.
